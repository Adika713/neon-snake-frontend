How to Play Neon Snake

Welcome to Neon Snake! Guide your snake to eat apples, grow longer, and achieve the highest score.

  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to control the snake's direction.
  • Eat red apples to grow and increase your score by 10 points.
  • Avoid hitting walls or your own tail, or the game ends.
  • Choose a difficulty:
    • Easy: Slow speed (5 fps), great for beginners.
    • Medium: Moderate speed (10 fps), a balanced challenge.
    • Hard: Fast speed (15 fps), for expert players.
  • Enter your name (e.g., "AirFalcon") to track your high score on the leaderboard.
  • The leaderboard updates only when you beat your personal high score and shows the top 10.
